Who We Are

We are volunteers from around the world united by our belief in Ukraine's victory

Victory for Ukraine

We believe in Ukraine's complete victory and the restoration of its territorial integrity within internationally recognized borders.

No Rewards for Aggression

Aggression must never be rewarded. Russia's invasion cannot result in territorial gains or political concessions.

Justice Over Crime

War criminals must be held accountable. We document and advocate for justice for all crimes committed against Ukraine.

International Boundaries

Respect for international law and sovereign borders is the foundation of global peace and security.
